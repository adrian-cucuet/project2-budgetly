    How Budget App Works

    First enter the Income Amount. It needs to be a positive number.

    For Expenses, mae sure you enter the name of the expense (grocery, electricity, pub, etc) and the correct amount for each expense.

    If you want to edit one of the expenses you can click on the Edit icon () and the name and amount of the expenses are gonna go back to the Expenses Form where they can be edited and re-submited.

    To delete an expense from the list, simply pres the Delete icon ().

    How To Do List Works

    Click on the form field and write your task. After you are done with the task text, press the plus icon ().

    When you are done with the task you can either delete it pressing on the Delete icon (), or you can mark it as done pressing on Done icon ().

    Currently you cannot edit a task, but I am working on bringing that option on a future update.
